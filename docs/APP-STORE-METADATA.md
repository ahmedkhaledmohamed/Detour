## Description
Detour shows you places along your driving route, ranked by how little time they add to your trip.

Enter where you're going, pick what you need — coffee, food, gas, groceries, pharmacy, or EV charging — and instantly see every option sorted by detour time. "+2 min" beats "+15 min."

KEY FEATURES:

- Ranked by detour time: Every result shows exactly how many minutes it adds to your trip
- Max-detour slider: Set your time budget — "only show places adding less than 5 minutes"
- Forward-only results: Never see places behind you on the route
- Visual detour preview: Tap any result to see the A → B → C route on the map
- One-tap navigation: Open the route with your stop in Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze
- Six categories: Coffee, Food, Gas, Grocery, Pharmacy, EV Charging

PERFECT FOR:
- Daily commuters who want a quick coffee stop
- Parents picking up dinner on the way home
- Road trippers planning efficient stops
- Anyone who's ever thought "I wish I could find something good without going too far off my route"

Google Maps shows you what's nearby. Detour shows you what's worth the stop.

## TestFlight Setup Steps

1. Open Xcode → Product → Archive
   - Requires a valid Development Team ID in project.yml
   - Set DEVELOPMENT_TEAM to your Apple team ID

2. In App Store Connect (appstoreconnect.apple.com):
   - Create new app: "Detour — Search Along Route"
   - Bundle ID: com.ahmedkhaled.detour
   - SKU: detour-ios
   - Primary language: English (Canada)

3. Upload the archive via Xcode Organizer

4. In TestFlight tab:
   - Add beta testers via email or public link
   - Set beta app description and feedback email

5. Need before first TestFlight build:
   - App icon (already in asset catalog)
   - Export compliance: No (ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption = false, already set)
   - Privacy policy URL (create a simple one)
